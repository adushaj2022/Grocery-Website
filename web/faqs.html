FAQ's

Which stores can I order groceries from?

Answer: Our service is optimized to allow our users to choose from a personalized list of grocery stores in their vincity in order to allow for efficient service.

Can I cancel my order if I forgot to add/remove an item?<

Answer: You can cancel your order withtin 10 minutes after purchasing your order.

Will I be refunded if my order is not delivered?

Answer: In the rare occurance that your food is not delivered to your door, the user will receive a full refund after they submit an incomplete delivery form.

How long will it take for my groceries to be delivered?

Answer: Each delivery takes approximately 2-5 hours on average.

Who delivers my order to the door?

Answer: Each order is processed and delivered by one of our screened and background checked carriers.

Can I schedule what time exaclty I want my order to be delivered?

Answer: If a customer requests their order well before their desired/requested drop-off time, then the deliivery can be scheduled.

What is Goods N' Go return policy?

The return policy on Goods N' Go is that we will accept returns if you have the reciept and have returned it within 3 hours of the delivery unopened.

Can I purchase alcohol, tobacco, and lottery tickets?

We believe in keeping our children safe and without addiction. Because of this, we will ask for ID. when delivering your grocery shopping order that may contain age appropriate items. The person accepting the order will be required to show proof of age with either a valid, legal, State, Government or Military issued identification card. The legal age to purchase alcohol, tobacco and lottery games is dependent upon your state. As well, depending upon local laws of your delivery area, the purchase or transportation of alcohol, may not be available.

Can I use coupons on my grocery shopping and delivery order?

If we already have coupons for specific items that you are ordering, we will use those coupons to save you money on the purchase of your groceries and share the savings with you 50/50. For example, if we provide a coupon that is specific to an item on your order and that coupon is valued at $1.00, we will keep .50 cents of the savings and you will reap the savings of .50 cents on your grocery order. We both win!
