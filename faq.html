Frequenlty Asked Questions

Here are the most frequenlty asked questions submitted by our user:

Which stores can I order groceries from?

Answer: Our service is optimized to allow our users to choose from a personalized list of grocery stores in their vincity in order to allow for efficient service.

Can I cancel my order if I forgot to add/remove an item?

Answer: You can cancel your order within 10 minutes after purchasing your order

Will I be refunded if my order is not delivered?

Answer: In the rare occurance that your food is not delivered to your door, the user will receive a full refund after they submit an incomplete delivery form.

How long does it take for my groceries to be delivered?

Answer: Each delivery approximately takes 2-5 hours on average.

Who delivers the groceries to my door?

Answer: Each order is processed and delivered by one of our screened background checked carriers.

Can I schedule what time exaclty I want my order to be delivered?

Answer: If a customer requests their order well before their desired/requeseted drop-off time, then the delivery can be scheduled.
